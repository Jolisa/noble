Engage

“The Black people are regal and dignified, and we are our greatest support. To come out strong, we as a collective must choose to be noble.”
Governor Brown - Founder of NOBLE

Noble is devoted to changing the way that Blacks experience living in America. As an African American collective, many, our founder included, have anecdotes of experiences with harassment, dismisal in academic or corporate spaces, and having to remain all the while as "unintimidating" as possible. The first steps to combatting this is creating our own spaces and moving as a collective to make sure we are heard.

We have a number of events to support this goal. Look below to see what's scheduled next.
