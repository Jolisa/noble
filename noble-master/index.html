July 23, 2020 / Why the Bee?

A Hive in Building its Community

The bee, though underappreciated and unacknowledged is crucial to our survival on Earth and contributes endlessly to agriculture, society, and our general vitality. If the bee were to disappear, humans could only live for four more years before facing extinction. Like the bee, Black people nationally and internationally have contributed endlessly to the infrastructure and growth of societies to now be unacknowledged. Our strength lies in our voice and in the hive.

The Noble Brand
