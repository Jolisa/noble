What is the Hive?

The bee, though underappreciated and unacknowledged, is crucial to our survival on Earth and contributes endlessly to agriculture, society, and our general vitality. If the bee were to disappear, humans could only live for four more years before facing extinction. Like the bee, Black people nationally and internationally have contributed endlessly to the infrastructure and growth of societies to now be unacknowledged. Our strength lies in our voice and in the hive. If you care or are concerned about:

  • The betterment of the community
  • Leveraging black wealth in support of Black causes
  • Adressing and holding transgressors responsible for police brutality

Then the hive is for you.
